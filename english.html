Biography

Ila Sachani is a distinguished.Born in Amreli, Gujarat, on 19 of december 1895,she was a talented Kathiawar embroidery artisther. Ila Sachani's life mission was to break barriers and challenge societal perceptions about disability. Despite being born with a physical disability that affected her hands, she aimed to demonstrate that one can lead a fulfilling, productive life regardless of physical limitations.Over the years, Ila became known for her remarkable ability to use her legs to do embroidery, despite being physically disabled,contributing significantly to make significant contributions to the field of embroidery and arts.

Her early education took place at the National Institute of Orthopedically Handicapped (NIOH) in Kolkata, India., where she excelled in arts and crafts, particularly in drawing and embroidery.Following her education, Ila pursued a career in doing embroidery. She became widely recognized as "the leg artist"
