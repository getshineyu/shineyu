India's Contribution to Satellite Technology

GSAT and NAVIC: Revolutionizing Weather Prediction, Navigation, and Disaster Management

Role in Weather Prediction

GSAT for Weather Monitoring

GSAT satellites, especially GSAT-6, GSAT-7, and GSAT-9, are equipped with weather-monitoring payloads that help track atmospheric conditions. These satellites provide real-time data on weather patterns, including cloud formations, rainfall, temperature, and wind patterns. The information is crucial for predicting monsoons, cyclones, and other extreme weather events.

Enhanced Forecasting

GSAT satellites help meteorologists create more accurate weather forecasts, providing valuable information for farmers, aviation, and general public. The data from these satellites allows for better preparation for severe weather, saving lives and reducing economic damage.

Role in Navigation

NAVIC: India's GPS System

NAVIC (Navigation with Indian Constellation) is India's regional navigation satellite system. It provides accurate position information services over India and surrounding areas. NAVIC is an alternative to the global positioning system (GPS) and helps in navigation, disaster management, and search and rescue operations.

Improved Accuracy

NAVIC is designed to provide location accuracy of 20 meters, which is crucial for applications like navigation, tracking, and location-based services. It supports transportation, logistics, and personal navigation applications across India, including remote and difficult-to-reach areas.

Role in Disaster Management

Disaster Monitoring through GSAT

GSAT satellites play a vital role in disaster management by providing data for early warning systems during natural disasters like floods, cyclones, and earthquakes. The real-time satellite imagery helps authorities take prompt action and evacuate vulnerable regions.

NAVIC for Emergency Response

NAVIC's navigation system is used in emergency response and search-and-rescue operations. The satellite network helps locate stranded individuals or vehicles, ensuring quick assistance during natural calamities, accidents, or other disasters.
